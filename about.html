    About Us

    We believe in a world where you have total freedom to be you, without judgement. To experiment. To express yourself. To be brave and grab life as the extraordinary adventure it is. So we make sure everyone has an equal chance to order all the amazing foods they’re capable of – no matter who they are, where they’re from or what foods they'll like to eat. We exist to give you the confidence to eat whichever food you want to eat. Try us!

    Food for all

    Our audience (AKA you) is wonderfully unique. And we do everything we can to help you find your best food – so you can be confident we’ve got the perfect thing for you.

    People

    We have 100 suppliers who use 15 factories around the city. We’re serious about making sure every single person in that supply chain is safe at work, respected and earns a living wage. We set high ethical standards and support our suppliers to help them meet them. We developed a world-leading Ethical Trade Strategy in 2017 and priorities include: transparency; improving wages; health and safety; addressing and reducing modern slavery risks, and identifying and stopping child labour.
